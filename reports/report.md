# Case Study Report (Synthetic)

## Decision to support
Should we run an 8-week pilot offering Spotify-related rewards inside a Vitality-style program?

## Pilot design
- **Eligibility** is stratified-randomized (age band × plan type)
- **Adoption** depends on digital affinity + campaign exposure
- Measurement window: **4 weeks pre** and **8 weeks post**

## KPIs
- Adoption funnel: eligible → adopted → redeemed
- Engagement: steps, workouts, vitality points
- Retention proxy: churn flag (monthly)
